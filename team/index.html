On a more serious note, my name is Helena Diao and I'm in 2B Geological Engineering. I grew up in Vancouver, and own a bamboo plant named Bambi. If you have any questions about FYELC or life in general, please email me!

My name is Kevin McNamara and I am the Vice-President Finance of the Waterloo Engineering Society, and also in charge of sponsorship for FYELC. My goal for the conference is to minimize the cost to delegates, while still providing a top notch experience for our first year students. I'm currently in 3B Civil Engineering and I'm interested in pursuing a career in the structural wind engineering field.
