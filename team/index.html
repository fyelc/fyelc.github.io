Allyson Francis / Co-Chair

Hello Waterloo! I am in my fourth year of Mechanical Engineering, and I've been highly involved since my first year. I'm a past Engineering Society President, and currently still serve as a Senator and Governor for UW, the Academic Affairs Commissioner for the Federation of Students, and the Academic Commissioner for the Canadian Federation of Engineering Students. In these roles, I advocate on behalf of students and work closely with student leaders from engineering schools across the country, as well as Engineers Canada. I've also been involved with Women In Engineering, engineering recruitment events, orientation week, and have been a long-time supporter and director of the Engineering Society's first year services and mentorship program. In my spare time, I build robots, travel, take photos, devour articles, and spend time with my two big fluffy dogs. I hope to get to know as many students as possible from the class of 2020 before I graduate, so make sure you say hi!
