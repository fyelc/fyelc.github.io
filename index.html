What is FYELC?

This fall, the University of Waterloo Engineering Society is hosting its inaugural First Year Engineering Student Conference (FYELC). The purpose of this conference is to give first year students the ability to develop personally and professionally, and to learn more about how to be successful in leadership positions on campus and in the work place. By attending this leadership and professional development conference, first year engineering students will be given the tools necessary to succeed throughout their university career and beyond.

By attending this conference you will be able to meet over 100 other first year students as well as numerous upper year mentors and leaders in a unique setting. Through various sessions, guest speakers, and team events, students will have the ability to network and meet new people, learn about how to get involved on campus and take on leadership roles, as well as to gain tips for being successful academically and in the work place. By providing you with this knowledge and information early in their university career, the Engineering Society hopes you will feel more confident in your ability to get involved outside of the classroom and to succeed.
